Diagnosing Tree Diseases with Expertise

Trees can suffer from a range of diseases caused by fungi, bacteria, or pests. Arborists are trained to spot symptoms early and differentiate between various infections. They recommend treatments such as soil enhancements, chemical applications, or pruning infected areas. Addressing issues early can save your tree and prevent the spread to nearby vegetation.

Improving Soil and Root Conditions

Healthy trees start with healthy soil. Arborists conduct soil testing to measure pH levels, compaction, and nutrient content. If deficiencies are found, they may recommend aeration, fertilization, or mulching. These treatments rejuvenate root zones and improve water absorption, giving trees the foundation they need to thrive.
